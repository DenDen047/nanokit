## 一次出典（CS に特化した実践知）

- [Michael Black, Writing a good scientific paper (Medium)](https://medium.com/@black_51980/writing-a-good-scientific-paper-c0f8af480c91)
  CVPR / NeurIPS / SIGGRAPH 系の実践。**Nugget** / **Goal-Problem-Solution** / Mad Libs abstract /
  具体的な禁則句 / 図・数式・コードの一致 / Acronym / Teaser / Video。
  本 skill の CS 側の背骨。
- [Bill Freeman, How to write a good CVPR submission (MIT, 2014)](https://billf.mit.edu/sites/default/files/documents/cvprPapers.pdf)
  初稿の自己レビュー、Related work の立て方、Reviewer 視点。
- [Eamonn Keogh, How to do good research, get it published in SIGKDD and get it cited!](https://www.cs.ucr.edu/~eamonn/Keogh_SIGKDD09_tutorial.pdf)
  "So what?" 駆動、baseline の妥当化、アイデアを売る視点。
- [Peyton Jones, How to Write a Great Research Paper](https://simon.peytonjones.org/great-research-paper/)
  7 simple suggestions（CS 版）— "Don't wait: write", Related Work 末尾配置。

## 一般原理（古典）

- [Hoey, On the Surface of Discourse (1983)](https://www.researchgate.net/publication/49550490_On_the_Surface_of_Discourse)
  Problem-Solution Pattern (S-P-R-E) の原典。GPS はこの CS 向け呼称。
- [Nature: How to construct a summary paragraph](https://www.nature.com/documents/nature-summary-paragraph.pdf)
  1 段落圧縮版。Abstract のバリアント A の原典。
- [Whitesides, Writing a Paper (Adv. Mater. 2004)](https://advanced.onlinelibrary.wiley.com/doi/10.1002/adma.200400767)
  Outline-first method。"Write the outline of the paper before doing the research."
- [McEnerney, The Craft of Writing Effectively (UChicago)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vtIzMaLkCaM)
  Value > Clarity。「読者の問題を動かすことが論文の仕事」。
- [Gopen & Swan, The Science of Scientific Writing (1990)](https://www.americanscientist.org/blog/the-long-view/the-science-of-scientific-writing)
  Reader expectation / stress position / old-new 連鎖。
- [Mensh & Kording, Ten simple rules for structuring papers (PLOS CB 2017)](https://journals.plos.org/ploscompbiol/article?id=10.1371/journal.pcbi.1005619)
  1 段落 = 1 メッセージ、context-content-conclusion。
- [Pinker, Why Academics Stink at Writing (Chronicle 2014)](https://www.chronicle.com/article/why-academics-stink-at-writing/)
  Curse of knowledge。専門用語の初出処理の根拠。
- [Sword, Beware of Zombie Nouns (TED-Ed)](https://ed.ted.com/lessons/beware-of-nominalizations-aka-zombie-nouns-helen-sword)
  名詞化（`-tion`, `-ity`, `-ment`）の害。
- [McCarthy/Savage, Tips on how to write a great science paper (Nature 2019)](https://www.nature.com/articles/d41586-019-02918-5)
  Minimalism。「各段落を 25% 削る」の根拠。
- [Knuth / Larrabee / Roberts, Mathematical Writing](https://jmlr.csail.mit.edu/reviewing-papers/knuth_mathematical_writing.pdf)
  数式の散文化、notation の一貫性、句読点の扱い。
- [Zobel, Writing for Computer Science (Springer)](https://link.springer.com/book/10.1007/978-1-4471-6639-9)
  CS 執筆の包括的リファレンス（書籍）。
